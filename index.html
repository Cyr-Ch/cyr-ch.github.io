Cyrine Chaabani

AI Research & Engineering

I am a Co-founder & CTO at Wattzo, building AI-powered energy optimization platforms. Prior to this, I worked as an Applied Scientist at Microsoft, focusing on speech and sign language detection, and as a Founding Lead AI/ML Engineer at Xaver. I hold a Master's degree in Robotics, Cognition, and Intelligence from the Technical University of Munich and an Honors Degree in Technology Management from CDTM.

My research and work leverage foundation models for solving complex tasks through specialization and reinforcement learning. Beyond this, I am interested in knowledge-representation, online learning and graph neural networks.

Always feel free to reach out with things you find exciting.

Publications

1. Explainable Energy Communities with Generative AI
IEEE ISGT, 2024
Introduces a generative-AI interface for Renewable Energy Communities that enables natural-language queries over optimization and forecasting workflows. Demonstrates improved transparency and decision support in case studies of community energy management.
2. Signer Overlap in Sign Language Detection
Preprint, 2023
Shows that training/test signer overlap inflates performance in sign-language detection. Proposes non-overlapping splits and reports a measurable drop when overlap is removed, arguing for more realistic evaluation protocols.
3. Clean Text & Full-Body Transformer for Sign Language Translation
WMT22, 2022
Describes a system that couples full-body visual features with careful target-text cleaning to address large vocabulary and data scarcity in SLT. Achieves state-of-the-art results in the WMT'22 task with strong human evaluation.
